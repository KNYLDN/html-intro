The Sandwich.

Intended for 4 persons.

INGREDIENTS

  • 8 slices of crusty bread
  • 1/2 c. herb mayo
  • 1/4 c. Dijon mustard
  • 12 slices deli ham
  • 8 slices provolone
  • 1 arugula
  • 1 tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced

DIRECTIONS

  1. In a medium bowl whisk together mayonnaise, parsley, thyme, oil, and garlic.
  2. Spread 2 tablespoons herb mayo on 4 slices of bread.
    Spread 1 tablespoon mustard on the other 4 slices of bread.
    Top each mayo slice of bread with 3 slices of ham, 2 slices of provolone,
    arugula, a couple slices of tomato, and red onion.
    Top with remaining bread slices, mustard side down.
